def saxs.GuinierAnalyzer.determine_qmax (   self)

Iteratively perform the Guinier analysis and determine the best qmax.

The product of the maximum q-value included in the analysis and the determined Radius of Gyration should be ~1.3 for globular structures or ~1 for extended/rod-shaped structures. This method iteratively performs the fitting procedure, updating which q-values to include in order to get as close as possible to these values.
